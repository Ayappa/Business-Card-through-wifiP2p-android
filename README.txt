Business Card app

We are creating two important functionalities using Barcode scanner.

1. Through QR code scan, we are sharing digital business cards which will have Name, phone number and email to connect and socialize.

2. Through QR code scan, we are connecting with other users over WIFI and sharing messages and images

- The app will directly store the data of the scanned user once the user confirms in the "Contacts" of the user.
-The app will facilitate to share images with other users over WIFI
- The app will let users send messages till they are connected.
